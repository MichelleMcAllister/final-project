ABOUT THE FOUNDER

Paymaneh "Bibi" Khalili was born in Stanford, California as the second of two first-generation Iranian-American children in her family. At a very young age she grew an appreciation for standing out from the crowd and being comfortable in ones own skin. Bibi always found a way to be a leader in all her endeavors and took pride in not looking or acting like everyone else. DAMSF is Bibi's way to do everyting. In addition to creating events to cater to the growth of both artists and their immediate communities, DAMSF hosts an online boutique named MishMash Boutique, comprised of unique goods created by both rising and established artists.
